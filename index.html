Best Local Brews

Would you like an easier way to find the best breweries in an area?

Best Local Brews is designed to help find the best local breweries to visit, with a focus on quality.

Simply enter a location and a search radius, and the Best Local Brews app will find breweries in that area and rank them using their average beer score across their entire portfolio of beers rated on RateBeer.com.

RateBeer has been aggregating beer reviews from thousands of beer enthusiasts since 2000, and provides Best Local Brews a reliable benchmark of the quality and capability of each brewery it finds for you.

Our results let you quickly reference each brewery's location, hours of operation, RateBeer average score and portfolio of reviews, Google reviews, and directions to get there.
